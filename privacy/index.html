Information We Collect

All DevTools operate entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools. No cookies are set by the tools themselves.

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. This data is handled entirely by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or visit Google Ad Settings to opt out of personalized advertising.

Data Processing

All tool processing happens client-side via JavaScript. Your JSON input, CSV output, and any other information never leaves your device. There are no server uploads, no logs, and no analytics tracking of your usage.

Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense for ad personalization and frequency capping.
