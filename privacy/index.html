information we collect

All WhetKit tools operate entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools. No cookies are set by the tools themselves.

third-party services

We use GoatCounter for privacy-focused analytics. GoatCounter does not use cookies and respects Do Not Track headers. No personal data is collected or stored.

In the future, we may display ads via Google AdSense. If activated, AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve relevant ads. This data would be handled by Google, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

data processing

All tool processing happens client-side via JavaScript. Your JSON input, CSV output, and any other information never leaves your device. There are no server uploads, no logs, and no analytics tracking of your usage.

cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. GoatCounter analytics does not use cookies. If Google AdSense is activated, third-party cookies may be set for ad personalization.

changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
